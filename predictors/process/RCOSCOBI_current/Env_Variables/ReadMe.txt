This folder has RCO-SCOBI-based environmental layers for 
  chlorophyll-a concentration
  nitrate concentration
  oxygen
  phosphate
  salinity
  temperature
  sea ice concentration
  sea ice thickness
  significant wave
calculated as mean of annual means and dtandard deviation of annual means for  period 2010-2023 for surface (0-3 m) and bottom (bottom + 3 m) layers for 250 m resolution.
